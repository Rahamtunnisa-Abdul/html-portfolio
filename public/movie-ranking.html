The Best Movies According to Raham

My top 3 movies of all-time are

  1. Baahubali 2-The Conclsuion
  2. RRR
  3. 3 Idiots

  1. Baahubali 2-The Conclusion

    2. Baahubali 2 is my favorite movie due to its epic storytelling, visually stunning sets, and powerful characters like Amarendra Baahubali and Devasena. The grandeur of its battle sequences, emotional depth, and iconic moments like Baahubali lifting the Shivalinga create a memorable cinematic experience. Its blend of action, drama, and mythological themes, alongside its cultural impact, make it a film that resonates deeply with many fans.

  2. RRR

    3. RRR is widely loved for its epic storytelling, stunning action scenes, and strong performances. The film reimagines two historical revolutionaries, Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju, creating a fictional tale of friendship and sacrifice. Directed by S.S. Rajamouli, it is a visual spectacle with incredible VFX and powerful music, especially the hit song "Naatu Naatu." Its blend of patriotism, action, and emotional depth makes it a favorite for many viewers.

  3. 3 Idiots

    4. 3 Idiots is a favorite for many because it blends humor, emotion, and life lessons in a way that resonates with audiences of all ages. The film, directed by Rajkumar Hirani, explores themes of friendship, self-discovery, and the pressures of the education system. It challenges societal expectations and encourages following one's passion instead of blindly chasing success.
